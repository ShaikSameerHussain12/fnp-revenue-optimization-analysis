# Problem Statement: Revenue Optimization & Demand Intelligence for FNP

## Business Context

FNP (Ferns N Petals) operates in a highly seasonal gifting market where customer demand fluctuates significantly based on occasions, time of day, and product categories. Despite steady order volume, revenue growth is inconsistent and marketing spend is not always aligned with demand patterns.

The leadership team is facing three core challenges:

* Revenue leakage due to poor demand forecasting
* Inefficient marketing spend during low-conversion periods
* Suboptimal product mix across categories and occasions

This project aims to analyze historical sales data and generate actionable insights to improve revenue, optimize operations, and increase customer spend.

---

## Key Business Problems

### 1. Revenue Concentration Risk

* Are we overly dependent on specific occasions (e.g., anniversaries, Raksha Bandhan) for revenue?
* What happens to revenue during non-peak periods?
* How can we smooth revenue across the year?

 Business Impact: High dependency increases volatility and risk. Identifying weak periods enables targeted campaigns to stabilize revenue.

---

### 2. Inefficient Product Portfolio

* Which product categories generate the highest revenue vs. lowest?
* Are low-performing products still consuming inventory and marketing budget?
* Should we expand or reduce certain categories?

 Business Impact: Optimizing product mix directly improves margins and reduces waste.

---

### 3. Seasonal Demand & Campaign Timing

* Which months generate peak revenue and which underperform?
* Are marketing campaigns aligned with actual demand spikes?
* When should we increase or decrease ad spend?

 Business Impact: Aligning campaigns with demand improves ROI and reduces unnecessary spend.

---

### 4. Customer Spending Behavior

* What is the average spend per customer?
* Are there opportunities to increase basket size (upselling/cross-selling)?
* Do high-value customers behave differently across occasions?

 Business Impact: Increasing average order value (AOV) is one of the fastest ways to boost revenue without increasing acquisition cost.

---

### 5. Order Fulfillment Efficiency

* What is the average order-to-delivery time?
* Does delivery time impact customer spending or order frequency?
* Are delays concentrated in certain periods or locations?

 Business Impact: Faster delivery improves customer satisfaction and repeat purchases.

---

### 6. Geographic Demand Distribution

* Which cities generate the highest number of orders?
* Are we underperforming in high-potential cities?
* Where should logistics and marketing investments be increased?

 Business Impact: Targeted expansion leads to better market penetration and higher ROI.

---

### 7. Time-Based Demand Patterns

* At what hours do customers place the most orders?
* Are we missing conversions during peak hours due to operational constraints?
* When should we run flash sales or push notifications?

 Business Impact: Capturing peak-hour demand increases conversion rates and revenue.

---

## Objective

To transform raw sales data into actionable business insights that:

* Increase revenue through better demand alignment
* Optimize marketing spend
* Improve operational efficiency
* Enhance customer value

---

## Expected Outcome

A data-driven strategy that enables FNP to:

* Maximize revenue during peak occasions
* Reduce revenue dips during off-season periods
* Improve product and category performance
* Increase average customer spend
* Optimize delivery and customer experience

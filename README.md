# fnp-revenue-optimization-analysis

# FNP Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

This project analyzes sales data from FNP to uncover patterns in customer behavior, product performance, seasonal demand, and operational efficiency.

The goal is not just reporting — it is to drive **revenue growth, cost optimization, and better business decisions** using data.

---

## What This Project Solves

Most dashboards stop at “what happened.”
This project answers:

* Why it happened
* What it means for the business
* What should be done next

---

## Key Insights & Business Value

### 1. Occasion-Based Revenue Strategy

* Revenue is heavily driven by specific occasions like anniversaries and Raksha Bandhan.
* Non-peak occasions show significant revenue drop.

 Action:

* Run targeted campaigns during low-demand occasions
* Introduce “micro-occasions” (self-gifting, friendship day, etc.)

 Impact:
Reduces revenue volatility and increases annual revenue consistency

---

### 2. Category Optimization

* Colors category dominates revenue
* Some categories contribute significantly less

 Action:

* Double down on high-performing categories
* Bundle low-performing products with bestsellers
* Reduce inventory for weak categories

 Impact:
Improves margins and inventory efficiency

---

### 3. Monthly Demand Trends (Seasonality)

* Revenue spikes during specific months (festival-driven)
* Clear low-demand months exist

 Action:

* Increase ad spend before peak months
* Reduce spend during low ROI periods
* Launch discount campaigns in off-season

 Impact:
Higher marketing ROI and better budget allocation

---

### 4. Customer Spending Behavior

* Average customer spend ≈ ₹2,700+

 Action:

* Introduce upselling (premium packaging, add-ons)
* Cross-sell complementary products
* Offer free delivery above a threshold

 Impact:
Increases Average Order Value (AOV) without increasing acquisition cost

---

### 5. Peak Hour Sales Optimization

* Orders spike at specific hours

 Action:

* Run flash sales during peak hours
* Send push notifications and email campaigns at high-conversion times
* Ensure system and logistics readiness during peak load

 Impact:
Maximizes conversions and prevents lost sales opportunities

---

### 6. Delivery Efficiency

* Average delivery time ~5.46 days

 Action:

* Optimize logistics in high-demand cities
* Improve last-mile delivery
* Prioritize faster fulfillment during peak occasions

 Impact:
Better customer experience → higher repeat purchases

---

### 7. City-Level Demand Insights

* Certain cities dominate order volume

 Action:

* Focus marketing in top-performing cities
* Expand operations in high-potential regions
* Customize campaigns regionally

 Impact:
Improved market penetration and localized growth
